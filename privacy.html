Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-06-02 · Last updated: 2026-06-02

This Privacy Policy describes how QuickClear ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the iOS application. By using QuickClear you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who we are

QuickClear is operated by Daniel Kholodov (the "Developer"), Germany. Contact: quickclearphotocleaner@gmail.com.

2. Summary

  • QuickClear processes your photos entirely on your device. Your photos never leave your iPhone.
  • We do not show advertising and we do not sell personal data.
  • We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated product-usage data so we can improve the App.
  • The only data exchanged with third parties is what Apple, RevenueCat, and Firebase need to process payments and provide aggregated analytics.

3. Photos and the Photo Library

QuickClear requests access to your Photo Library so you can review, organize, and delete your own photos. All photo processing — including any on-device AI grouping, duplicate detection, and image analysis — happens locally on your device using Apple's PhotoKit, Vision, and Core ML frameworks. Photos are never uploaded, copied to a server, or shared with the Developer or any third party.

Deletion of photos is performed through the standard iOS Photo Library APIs and is subject to your confirmation through the iOS system dialog.

4. Subscriptions and payments

QuickClear offers optional paid subscriptions. Purchases are processed exclusively through Apple's App Store using StoreKit. We use RevenueCat, Inc. as a subscription management provider to validate receipts and manage entitlements.

When you make a purchase, the following information may be processed by Apple and RevenueCat:

  • An anonymous app-specific user identifier
  • Your subscription status, product identifier, and purchase/expiration dates
  • Country/region and device type as reported by the App Store

We do not receive your name, email address, billing address, or payment card information. Apple's and RevenueCat's privacy policies apply to data they process:

  • Apple: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • RevenueCat: revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Local data stored on your device

The App stores the following locally on your device only, using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Your curation decisions (keep, delete, etc.) so the App remembers what you have reviewed
  • A staged deletion queue until you confirm deletion
  • Aggregated, anonymous usage counts used to enforce the free-tier limit
  • Your in-app preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App removes it.

6. Analytics

QuickClear uses Google Firebase Analytics (provided by Google Ireland Limited / Google LLC) to understand how the App is used in aggregate and to improve features and stability. Firebase processes:

  • Anonymous, app-instance-level event data (e.g. screen views, taps on features, subscription paywall views)
  • Device model, OS version, language/region, app version
  • An Firebase App Instance ID, which is reset when you reinstall the App or reset advertising identifiers
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (city-level only; the IP itself is not retained for analytics)

We have configured Firebase so that the Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA) is not collected, no Google Ads SDKs are integrated, and data sharing with Google products outside of Analytics is disabled. As a result, no App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt is required.

Firebase data may be processed on Google servers in the United States. Google relies on the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework and Standard Contractual Clauses as a legal basis for the transfer. See Google's policies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy and policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not show advertising inside the App and we do not engage in cross-app tracking.

7. Children

QuickClear is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your rights (GDPR / EU)

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, most data-subject rights under the GDPR are fulfilled by your control over the device. You retain the right to:

  • Revoke Photo Library access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos
  • Cancel your subscription via Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Delete all local app data by uninstalling QuickClear
  • Contact us with any privacy question at quickclearphotocleaner@gmail.com

For data processed by Apple or RevenueCat, please contact those providers directly.

9. California privacy rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (as amended by the CPRA) gives you the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, the right to correct it, and the right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information.

  • Categories collected: identifiers (Firebase App Instance ID), internet/electronic activity (app event data), geolocation data (approximate, IP-derived), and commercial information (subscription status).
  • Purpose: analytics, product improvement, subscription management.
  • "Sale" or "sharing": We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Retention: Firebase analytics data is retained per Google's default retention; subscription data is retained as long as your subscription is active plus as required by applicable accounting law.
  • Exercising your rights: email quickclearphotocleaner@gmail.com. We will verify your request using information reasonably available to us.

10. Security

Because data stays on your device and is protected by iOS sandboxing and encryption-at-rest, security primarily depends on the security of your device itself (passcode, Face ID/Touch ID, iOS updates).

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Material changes will additionally be communicated within the App.

12. Contact

Questions or requests: quickclearphotocleaner@gmail.com
